Introduction to Earn to Die

Earn to Die is an exhilarating online game that immerses players in a thrilling zombie apocalypse scenario. With over 200 million plays, this revamped version offers an enhanced experience for mobile and tablet users. Players find themselves stranded in a desert, armed only with a dilapidated car and limited cash, facing the daunting task of driving through relentless waves of zombies to survive.

Key Features of Earn to Die

  • Extended Story Mode: Engage in a brand new story that takes you across the country during the zombie apocalypse.
  • Diverse Vehicle Selection: Choose from 8 unique vehicles, including race cars, trucks, and even school buses.
  • Extensive Upgrade Options: Customize each vehicle with powerful upgrades such as guns, boosters, and spiked frames to enhance your zombie-slaying capabilities.
  • Zombie Mayhem: Experience the thrill of crashing into zombies with a realistic ragdoll physics engine that sends them flying.
  • Challenging Game Modes: Compete for the best time in Championship Mode or enjoy festive challenges in Halloween Mode.

Gameplay Mechanics

In Earn to Die, players must strategically navigate through hordes of zombies while earning cash to upgrade their vehicles. The gameplay combines elements of action, driving, and vehicle customization, making it a unique experience. As players progress, they unlock new vehicles and enhancements that transform their rides into ultimate zombie-slaying machines.

Controls

To get started in Earn to Die, simply press W or UP to accelerate and begin your journey through the undead-infested wasteland.
